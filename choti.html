CHOTI CHIDIYAN..

kabhi kabhi choti chidiyon ko lgta h
wo bahot smjhdaar h

unhone abhi abhi duniya mein kadam hi rkha h

unhe to sb apna lgta h

chidiyan bhi kya kre abhi kadam jo rkha h

uspe jab koi bandishe lgti h

uski azaadi chin rhi h

par tu bhi kya kre
abhi abhi duniya m jo kadam rkha h...
