YEH BAARISH RUK KYUN NHI RHI AAJ...

yeh baarish ruk kyun nhi rhi aaj
aaj kya hua h ishe
kya yeh bahot khush h
shayad iski khoi cheez mil gyi
yaa yeh koi gum ko chupa rha h
apni choti choti bundo mein
apni har gum ko bhula rha h
yaa khud ko kisike k liye tarpa rha h
tera yun ruk ruk k brsna
teri naarazgi dikha rha h

tu naaraz na hua kr
kisi ek k liye
apno ko yun baichain naa kiya kr
toh kya hua woh kho gya
tere liye toh pua aashmaan
apni baahein faila rkha h
puri dharti teri har ek bund ko
chumne baithi hui h
pura samandar tere liye
khud lahro se dgha kiye baitha h

tere liye peron ne
khud ko jhuka rkhka h
phulon neh khilna bhula rkha h
chor us ek bewfa ko
kyun uske liye itno ko rula rkha h
yeh baarish ruk kyun nhi rhi aaj...

..Prity singh
