TUM AAOGE NAH..

tum aaoge nah
aaj ka din mere liye bahot khaas h
pahli baar apni shauq ko
apni kalpnaon ko
apni baaton ko
apne bejan pare alfaazon ko
ek aayan de rhi hun
unhe naam de rhi hun
tum aaoge nah...

jaanti hun whan bheed hogi
laakhon ki nazar hogi
kayi baatein hogi
kuch achii hongi
kuch buri hongi
kabhi shor hoga
kabhi shaanti hogi
kabhi taaliyan bajegi
kabhi taarif hogi
par suno nah
tum aaoge nah..

aaj m khud ki puri hongi
khud ki baatein krungi
apni afshaane sunaongi
apni jajbaaton ko byaan krungi
khud ki kalpnaon ko pankh dungi
apne khubshurat palon ki smritiyan bnaungi
par suno nah
tum aaoge nah...

..Prity singh
